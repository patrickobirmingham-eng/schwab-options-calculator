📖 How to export your transactions from Schwab

  1. Log in to Schwab.com and go to Accounts → Transaction History
  2. Select your options account and set your desired date range, then click the Search button
  3. Click the Export button (top right of the transactions table)
  4. Choose CSV format and download
  5. Upload that file here — no edits needed

💡 Tip: Export up to 2 years at a time for best results.

📖 How to export your Realized Gain / Loss from Schwab

  1. Log in to Schwab.com and go to Accounts → Realized Gain / Loss
  2. Select your options account and set your desired date range, then click the Search button. Be sure the Options filter is set to "Include options contracts"
  3. Click the Export button (top right of the transactions table)
  4. Choose Export Details Only and download
  5. Upload that file here — no edits needed

💡 Tip: This is a different export from the main transactions file — use the Gain/Loss tab specifically.
